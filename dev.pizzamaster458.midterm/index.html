Why Helicopters are better than Drones

-Pizzamaster458(Aka Jack MacKinnon)

Why are helicopters better than drones? They are able to outmanuver and outrun
any existing drone in the market, Helicopters are considerably faster than any drone that
can carry a similar camera payload. They can gain altitude much faster than drones and do manuvers
simply not possible with traditional drones. That is why there will always be helicopters in cinema
because a lot of shots need the speed and altitude of a helicopter to achieve the desired look.

But Drones are safer?

While it is true that drones are safer, the story is larger than that. Helicopters rarely have accidents
and when they do they are usually survivable. The risk argument is invalid in my opinion. Pilots are much more highly
qualified in helicopters than drones, leading to an overall very safe track record. The risk does exist, but it is negligible.
