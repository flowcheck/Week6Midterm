Why Drones are better than Helicopters

-Jack MacKinnon

Why are drones better than helicopters? Well drones are better
than helicopters for a number of reasons. First, they have no risk
as a crew isn't needed to operate one on board.
Much less expensive to operate, can be operated indefinitely with batteries,
Smaller, more outlets for creativity with close proximity to obstacles.

But Helicopters Produce Better Footage?

Not always the case, larger cameras can be fitted to large drone platforms
producing helicopter-like shots for a fraction of the cost, risk and time. These
drones can be carried on-site by backpack, as they are lightweight and easy to set up.
The drone market is here to stay, and helicopters are becoming more and more obsolete
in modern day filmaking. Some helicopter capabilities outweigh the drones. Such as speed
and altitude. But those are improvements that are constantly being pushed in drone technology.
3 Main reasons drones are better than Helicopters:

  • Safety
  • Versitility
  • Consistency
